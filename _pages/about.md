## About Me
I am a **Master** graduate student of Artificial Intelligence, Optics and Electronics ([iOPEN](http://iopen.nwpu.edu.cn/)), Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), supervised by [Qi Wang](https://crabwq.github.io/), [Yuan Yuan](http://iopen.nwpu.edu.cn/info/1015/1389.htm) and [Xuelong Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ahUibskAAAAJ) professors. I received **Bachelor** degree at School of Automation, Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU). My research area includes computer vision, deep learning, image/video enhancement learning and scene text recognition etc.


I am currently working on cloud computing research and development at Huawei, and according to my plan, I am going to find a research group to continue working on artificial intelligence related algorithms and pursue a **Ph.D**. If you are interested in me, please send e-mail to me. Thanks a lot!
