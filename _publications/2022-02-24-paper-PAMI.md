This paper proposed a large-scale video based license plate dateset named LMV-LP and a baseline (License Plate Recognition) LPR method on this dataset. 
We extend research in this field from a novel perspective, and the specific contributions are summarized as follows:
* A large-scale and multi-source video based LPDR dataset, namely LMV-LP, is proposed, which contains more than 400k video frames with various complex scenes and diverse data sources; 
* A tool for labeling vehicle information in videos is released, and multiple people can collaborate to develop
labeling; 
* A novel framework based on video datasets is proposed, which can use inter-frame features to improve the recognition effect of each frame under the premise of satisfying speed. Multi-scale feature fusion is applied in the detection and recognition stages; 
* The experiments of various SOTA algorithms on LMV-LP verify the necessity of this dataset and the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm.


**My work:** main idea, conduction of experiments, and paper writing.
