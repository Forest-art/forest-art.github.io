## Task
Design circuit boards and program programming on the designated car model, and complete the car model's self-service cruise on the race track to complete a lap. The two-car chase group requires two cars to chase after themselves. They cannot go out of bounds or collide. If they complete the overtaking task, they will receive a 5s time reward.


## Our Team
We used inductance for cruising, successfully completed 5 overtaking tasks in the competition, maintained high stability in the final, and finally won the top1.



## Improvement
* Using double-row 8-inductor for path extraction, it perfectly achieves accurate identification and special processing of each element.
* Using four-layer circuit board design, it has extremely high stability.
* A unique cross element overtaking mission is designed, which is strategically dominant.
